Our Story

The Clean Services story began back in 1999 with the ambition to provide accommodation providers with an extensive and innovative range of products to help operate their business – in short, everything they needed for great hospitality!

With a wealth of experience from running hospitality businesses of our own, we understand many of the challenges faced every day by people managing hotels, running B&Bs and guest houses, or operating holiday lettings and self-catering cottages.

Today, Clean Services is managed by a leadership team with extensive experience across hospitality, retail, logistics and supply chain businesses.

Our Commitment

As well as offering over 2,000 products in our range, we are dedicated to making the ordering process as easy as possible for you, whether you choose to buy here on our website or, if you prefer, placing your order with our Customer Services Team.

From all of us, thank you for visiting our website and we hope you're delighted with the selection of quality products available.

Expert Support

An Ethical Vision

As a values-led organisation our aim is to be an ethical, helpful and profitable company by valuing our customers, nurturing our team and by being involved in the local, national and global community.

More than mere words, we put these values into practice every day. Clean Services will never be a purely profits-driven business, and we prefer to provide high quality products at a fair price to our customers, sourced as ethically and sustainably as possible, with the impact on the environment always a consideration.

This also extends to people, both our own employees and the welfare of those employed by our suppliers.
